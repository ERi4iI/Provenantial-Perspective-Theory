##### The Aristotelian Circle

**Aristotle's four causes:**

1. **Material cause** — what something is made of
2. **Formal cause** — the form/pattern/essence
3. **Efficient cause** — the agent of change, the "how"
4. **Final cause** — the purpose, the "why," **telos**

**What happened historically:**

-  Pre-Socratics: focused on material (water, air, atoms)
-  Socrates/Plato: focused on formal (Forms, ideals)
-  Aristotle: **all four, integrated**
-  Then the West took a deal: science got efficient cause, theology got final cause
-  The hostage situation: telos was traded for autonomy
